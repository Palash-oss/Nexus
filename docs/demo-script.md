# Nexus Demo Script (2 minutes)

## Opening (0:00–0:10)
Show the landing page loading at nexus.app.
Hover over the features section briefly.
Narrate: "Finding things across your apps is broken. Every tool has its own search. Nexus fixes that."

## Sign In (0:10–0:25)
Click "Get Early Access" → redirected to /login
Click "Continue with Google" → OAuth flow → redirect to /search
Narrate: "Sign in with Google in 5 seconds."

## Indexing (0:25–0:40)
Click "Connect & Index" button.
Show the progress indicator as emails and Drive files are indexed.
Narrate: "Nexus pulls in your Gmail and Google Drive automatically."

## First Search — Keyword (0:40–0:55)
Type: "project proposal"
Show results from Gmail and Drive appearing together, fast.
Click one result — it opens Gmail in a new tab.
Narrate: "One search bar. All your apps."

## Second Search — Semantic AI (0:55–1:20)
Type: "that invoice Rahul sent last month"
Show results appear — the right email is #1.
Show the AI summary under it.
Show the ParsedQuery interpretation: "Searching Gmail · filtered to November"
Narrate: "Nexus understands what you mean. Not just what you type."

## Extension (1:20–1:40)
Switch to Chrome extension popup. Show pages indexed.
Search for: "rust ownership tutorial"
Show a webpage from the user's browsing history as a result.
Narrate: "The browser extension captures pages as you browse. Find anything you've ever read."

## Close (1:40–2:00)
Return to landing page.
Show the waitlist form.
Narrate: "Nexus. Find anything. Everywhere. Instantly. Join the waitlist."
