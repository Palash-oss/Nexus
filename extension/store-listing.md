# Nexus Search — Web Extension Store Listing

## Title
Nexus Search — Search Everything

## Short Description
Search your Gmail, Drive, and browser history in one place. Find anything in seconds.

## Long Description
Nexus is a universal search layer for your personal knowledge. This extension captures the pages you visit and indexes them securely in your Nexus account, making your browsing history fully searchable alongside your emails and cloud files.

### Key Features:
* **Background Indexing**: Silently capture and index text from articles and documents you read.
* **Auto-Deduplication**: Never index the same page twice. Uses smart 24-hour deduplication caches.
* **Secure Blocklists**: Sensitive pages (like bank accounts, login pages, and social media) are automatically filtered out.
* **Pause Control**: Toggle background syncing with a single click.
